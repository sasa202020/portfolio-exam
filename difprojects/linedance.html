It's a lot of fun and honetsly so easy to learn. It's just the best relax that helps you clear your mind, engage with other peole and make new friends. I'm always looking forward the dance session because I know I will come home happier.

What is technique of the line dance

It has some set of rules just like any other dance style and basic steps. Once you master these then you should have no issues grasping even the most difficult choreography. It's all about building your muscle memory, musicality and getting good coordination - these are strong foundations that are important for any kind of dance really

How do you choose music for dances?

Whatever makes my body move is a good way to go! Sometimes when I'm not sure I do the "crowd test" when I play the song during a party and try to see how the crowd responds to it - if they go crazy I know that it will create a good energy in the dance as well. I try to change it up a bit and sometimes go for current banger, other times going for a nostalgic hit from early 2000s or the 90s.
